Chicken Soup

This recipe is our go-to for lazy sundays lunches.

Ingredients

  • 1kg chicken mix
  • Whatever vegetables you want, including: tomatoes, potatoes, onions, pepper, carrots etc.
  • Parsley, lovage, dill
  • Salt, pepper, borscht

Steps

  1. Properly clean the chicken
  2. Add the chicken into a bowl of water and let it boil for 1 hour
  3. Cut the vegetables into cubes and mince the spices
  4. Remove the chicken from the broth and remove the bones
  5. Add the vegetables to the broth and wait for them to soften
  6. Add the meat back and add the spices and the borscht. Boil for another 15 minutes
  7. Enjoy!
